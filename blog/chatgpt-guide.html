What is ChatGPT and How to Use It

A beginner-friendly guide · 5 min read

ChatGPT is an AI chatbot built by OpenAI that can hold conversations, answer questions, write content, and help with everyday tasks. You simply type what you need in plain language, and it responds instantly with text — no special commands or technical knowledge required.

Since its launch, ChatGPT has become one of the most widely used AI tools in the world, used by students, professionals, and businesses for everything from drafting emails to learning new topics.

What can ChatGPT actually do?

ChatGPT is flexible enough to help with a wide range of tasks, including:

  • Writing and editing emails, essays, and social media posts
  • Explaining difficult concepts in simple language
  • Brainstorming ideas for projects, business names, or content
  • Summarizing long articles or documents
  • Helping with basic coding and debugging
  • Translating text between languages

How to get started

Getting started with ChatGPT takes less than two minutes:

  • Go to the ChatGPT website and create a free account using an email address
  • Once logged in, you'll see a simple chat box
  • Type your question or request in everyday language and press enter
  • ChatGPT will respond, and you can continue the conversation by asking follow-up questions

Tips for better results

The quality of ChatGPT's response depends a lot on how you ask. A few tips:

  • Be specific — instead of "write an email," try "write a polite email to my landlord asking for a repair"
  • Give context — mention your audience, tone, or purpose
  • Ask it to revise — if the first answer isn't quite right, ask for changes
  • Break big tasks into smaller steps for more focused answers

Free vs paid plans

ChatGPT offers a free version that covers most everyday needs. There's also a paid subscription that unlocks faster responses, access to more advanced models, and additional features like image generation and file uploads — useful for heavier or professional use.

Final thoughts

ChatGPT is a great starting point for anyone curious about AI — it's free, easy to use, and useful for almost any task involving text. The best way to learn what it can do for you is simply to start chatting and experimenting with different requests.
